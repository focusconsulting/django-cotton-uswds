Bordered

To apply for a federal grant, visit Grants.gov to search for available funding opportunities. Each listing includes eligibility requirements, application deadlines, and submission instructions. You will need to create an account and register your organization before submitting an application.

Required documents typically include a project narrative, budget justification, organizational capability statement, and any required certifications. Specific requirements vary by program, so review the Notice of Funding Opportunity carefully for a complete list.

Review timelines vary by agency and program. Most agencies will notify applicants within 90 to 180 days after the application deadline. You can check the status of your application through your Grants.gov account at any time.

Multiselectable

To be eligible for this program, applicants must be U.S. citizens or permanent residents, at least 18 years of age, and have a valid Social Security number. Additional requirements may apply depending on the specific benefit.

Applications can be submitted online through our secure portal or in person at your local field office. Processing times are typically 4 to 6 weeks. You will receive a confirmation number upon successful submission.

If your application is denied, you have 60 days from the date of the decision letter to file an appeal. Appeals must be submitted in writing and include any additional documentation that supports your case.

For questions about your application or eligibility, contact our help desk at 1-800-555-0199 between 8:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m. ET, Monday through Friday. You can also email us at support@agency.gov.
