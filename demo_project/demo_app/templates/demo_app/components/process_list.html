Review the program requirements to confirm you meet all eligibility criteria. You must be a U.S. citizen or legal permanent resident, at least 18 years of age, and meet the income guidelines for your household size.

  • Check income limits for your state
  • Confirm residency requirements
  • Review disqualifying factors

Collect the required documents before starting your application. Having these ready will help you complete the process more quickly.

  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Social Security card or number
  • Proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns)
  • Proof of residency (utility bill, lease agreement)

Complete and submit your application online through our secure portal, by mail, or in person at your local office. Online applications are typically processed faster. You will receive a confirmation number upon submission.

After your application is reviewed, you may be scheduled for an eligibility interview. Interviews can be conducted by phone or in person. Bring all original documentation to your interview appointment.

You will be notified of the decision by mail within 30 days of your interview. If approved, your benefits will begin on the first day of the following month. If denied, the letter will explain the reason and your right to appeal.
